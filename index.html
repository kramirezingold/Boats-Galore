Why Boats Galore

Southwest Michigan's Trusted Storage for High-Value Vehicles

Mike and Leslie Malin have been protecting their neighbors' boats, RVs, and recreational vehicles from Michigan's harsh winters right here in Dowagiac. Conveniently located near Sister Lakes, Boats Galore offers the kind of personalized, reliable service you simply won't find at a big storage chain.

Every vehicle goes inside — out of the snow, ice, and UV — in a secure, well-lit facility you can access any time of day or night.
